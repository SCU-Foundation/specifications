# Contributing — Lattice Meta Specification (LM‑Spec)

**Specification:** Lattice Meta Specification (LM‑Spec)  
**Version:** v1.0  
**Steward:** SCU Foundation  
**Research Body:** Lattice Research Institute (LRI)  
**Repository:** REPO: Specifications — /specifications/lm/

---

## Purpose

This document defines the contribution rules for LM‑Spec.  
All contributions MUST follow the SCU Foundation’s governance, safety, meta‑semantic boundary, and change‑management processes.

---

## Contribution Principles

Contributors MUST:

- follow governance and change‑management rules  
- maintain neutrality and avoid proprietary or commercial influence  
- ensure alignment with canonical repository structure  
- respect contextual‑meaning, alignment, safety, and provenance boundaries  
- provide clear, evidence‑based proposals  
- avoid ambiguity, drift, or undocumented changes  
- follow update sequences defined in governance‑root documents  

---

## Contribution Process

1. **Proposal Preparation**  
   - describe the change, rationale, and expected impact  
   - ensure alignment with Repo‑Map vE1.2 and GRM vE1.1  
   - confirm no violation of contextual‑meaning or safety rules

2. **Submission**  
   - submit via pull request or governance‑approved channel  
   - include references to governance‑root documents  
   - ensure canonical formatting and repo‑scoped referencing

3. **Review**  
   - conducted by SCU Foundation and, where relevant, LRI  
   - assesses correctness, neutrality, safety, and structural alignment  
   - clarification may be requested

4. **Approval or Rejection**  
   - SCU Foundation makes final decisions  
   - approved changes merged according to governed sequencing  
   - rejected changes include rationale and guidance

---

## Prohibited Contributions

- proprietary or commercial content  
- ambiguous or incomplete proposals  
- bypassing governance or update sequences  
- violating safety, contextual‑meaning, or provenance rules  
- attempts to redefine governance or institutional roles
